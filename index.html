About Veronica

A Letter to Families

With over 26 years of experience and having supported more than 1,000 families, Veronica offers compassionate, expert, and deeply personalized care during one of the most transformative seasons of life. Based in Los Angeles and Long Beach, she serves families locally and internationally as a Traveling Baby Nurse and International Doula, providing continuity, reassurance, and skilled support wherever the journey unfolds.

She specializes in newborn care, preemies, and twins, and proudly supports LGBT families, as well as families welcoming babies through birth, adoption, or surrogacy. Her services also extend to families using CARROT benefits and participating in the Bridge Program, ensuring access to high-quality support and guidance. Veronica walks alongside parents with calm presence, clear guidance, and trauma-informed care.

Her work centers on hands-on coaching that empowers parents to feel confident—from feeding support (breastfeeding, chest-feeding, or bottle-feeding) to understanding their baby’s cues and rhythms. As an Infant Sleep Coach, she helps families establish healthy, gentle sleep foundations.

Every family receives a customized care plan designed to nurture the baby, support parental recovery, and strengthen confidence—so you can begin this chapter feeling grounded, connected, and supported.

Nest and rest, while she takes care of the rest.

You deserve the best.

With warmth and dedication,

Your Doula, Veronica
